Development

Tailored solutions give clients the reliability, quality and support that is vital in today's environment. Whether you need a standalone application on a Windows or Unix system, or a complete integration solution based on heavy duty Java Application Servers providing REST services as part of a modern mobile solution, my experience providing solutions in all environments along that spectrum will save you the trouble of re-inventing the wheel and avoid painful lessons.

Integration Consultancy

Organisations often have a myriad of systems acting as stand alone repositories holding information vital to more than just one part of the business.

Integration allows your organisation to treat these disparate data sources, either by data migration or through automated data exchange as a single, cohesive data source. Integration allows you to increase the speed of individual transactions, gives faster reporting turn-around and more importantly, vastly reduces data quality issues by automating application interactions.

In some cases it will be necessary to review and modify existing code to meet your integration needs. With many years in software development you can rest assured any code changes made as part of your project will be seamless.

System Analysis, Deployment and Support

Upgrades, moves to alternative technologies or the introduction of new components or infrastructure can affect existing systems. Using the experience I have accumulated through vastly different projects I can help analyse the impact such a move will have on your current processes. This will help to identify what changes are needed within your organisation to support the move.
